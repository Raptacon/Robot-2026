# While running code, there are bound to be errors which require the entire program to be terminated. These are all possible codes *exclusive to intake* along with what they mean and possible conditions which caused it
# Note that there may be more conditions which caused a fault, these are merely suggestions.

- ERR 101: Unsuccessful DEPLOYMENT OF INTAKE within expected time; Motor may not moving / failing to move within expected time
- ERR 102: Unsuccessful STOW OF INTAKE within expected time; Motor may not moving / failing to move within expected time
- ERR 103: Unsuccessful ACTIVATION OF ROLLERS within expected time; Motor may not moving / failing to move within expected time
- ERR 104: Unsuccessful DEACTIVATION OF ROLLERS within expected time; Motor may not moving/ failing to move within expected time
- ERR 105: Unsuccessful REVERSAL OF ROLLERS CORRECTING JAMMED BALL within expected time; Motor may not moving / failing to move within expected time

- ERR 112: INTAKE motor does not appear to be moving

- ERR 121: DEPLOYMENT OF INTAKE MOTOR appears to be moving outside limits
- ERR 122: STOWING OF INTAKE MOTOR appears to be moving outside limits
